OBJECTIVE


Making technology more accessible and easier to use is the driving principle connecting my work as a software developer and technical writer. It’s pushed me to pursue projects, build tools, and write content that caters to people from various backgrounds (tech and non-tech), skill levels, and neurotypes.


While I believe in writing clear, self-documenting code, I recognize it isn’t always enough to let the code speak for itself. As such, I also believe it’s important that software should be well-annotated and accompanied by comprehensive, well-written documentation.


Creating usage guides, API documentation, and managing knowledge bases aren’t tasks that most software developers are equipped to perform. But they’re vital aspects of the software’s life-cycle and existence.


Having a significant background in development enables me to untangle the technical nuances of software and IT, so I can effectively translate and communicate them to other developers and end users.


I’m looking to collaborate with a company that can help me leverage my considerable experience and skill to improve user experiences with technology.
